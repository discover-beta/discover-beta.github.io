About Us

At Discover, we're bringing predictive analytics to social events. We provide a mobile platform for people to find events tailored to their interests on demand. The target date for our beta launch is October 2016!

Our Technology

Discover is leveraging Machine Learning to disrupt the entertainment industry.


Decision Tree Learning

We use decision trees as predictive models to easily bucket users into shared interest groups. Fun stuff.

GPS Navigation System

Track where social events are relative to you. With one tap, find suggested routes to get you to where you want to go.

Social Media Analytics

We observe our user's social media profiles and extract data to personalize their experiences with Discover.
